Ring of Mystery

Vivaldi's Ring of Mystery is an audio production from Classical Kids that tells the story of Katarina, a young violinist who comes under the tutelage of Antonio Vivaldi. The Ring of Mystery contains many excerpts from Vivaldi's most famous works.
